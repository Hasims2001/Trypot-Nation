India is a great destination for scuba diving enthusiasts with multiple diving spots throughout the country.

Some of the best scuba diving spots in India include Netrani Island in Karnataka, Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Lakshadweep, Goa, and Pondicherry. Netrani Island in Karnataka is known for its beautiful reefs, while Andaman and Nicobar Islands offer stunning coral reefs and crystal clear waters.
