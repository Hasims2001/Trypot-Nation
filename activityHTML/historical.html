About

India is home to numerous historical places that attract tourists from all over the world. Many of these places are listed as UNESCO World Heritage sites due to their cultural importance.

The Taj Mahal in Agra is a famous historical place and is considered one of the seven wonders of the world. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan for his queen Mumtaz. There are also many other famous historical places in India, including the Agra Fort, Red Fort, Qutub Minar, Fatehpur Sikri, Hawa Mahal, Victoria Mahal, Humayun's Tomb, Khajuraho Temples, Jallianwala Bagh, Sanchi Stupa, Meenakshi Amman Temple, Golden Temple, and Elephanta Caves, among others.
